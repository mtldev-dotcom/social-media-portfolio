# Voice & Tone

Global writing rules for all content on the site.

---

## Rules

- Short sentences.
- Calm confidence.
- No buzzwords.
- Write like you talk.
- Lowercase allowed.
- Honest > impressive.

---

## Examples

Good:
- "i build calm systems."
- "this didn't work. here's what i learned."
- "available to chat."

Bad:
- "Leveraging cutting-edge AI solutions..."
- "Revolutionizing the way teams operate..."
- "Full-stack thought leader and innovator."
